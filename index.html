Welcome to KV AFS Begumpet

Where Learning Meets Character

Established in 1982 on the serene campus of the Air Force Station Begumpet in Hyderabad, our Vidyalaya has grown into a prestigious co-educational institution. We follow the CBSE curriculum, helping children soar to new heights under the administrative guidance of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS).

"तत् त्वं पूषन् अपावृणु" "O Sun, please remove the covering of ignorance to reveal the Truth." — Motto of Kendriya Vidyalaya

Secure Green Campus

Holistic Growth

Our Pathways

Senior Secondary Streams (+2 Level)

We offer three comprehensive academic streams designed to equip students for higher education and diverse global careers.

Science & Technology

Science Stream

Focuses on advanced mathematics, computing, and laboratory experiments. Prepares students for elite engineering, medical research, and technological fields.

  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology Labs
  • Mathematics & Informatics Practices
Finance & Economics

Commerce Stream

Centered on business management, financial statements, and macroeconomics. Prepares future chartered accountants, corporate managers, and entrepreneurs.

  • Accountancy & Business Studies
  • Economics & Applied Mathematics
Humanities & Social Sciences

Humanities Stream

Explores history, human geography, civic institutions, and languages. Fosters critical reasoning, policy understanding, and prep for public services (UPSC) and legal careers.

  • History, Geography & Political Science
  • Creative Writing & Seminar Programs
Admissions Information

Begin Your Journey With Us

Admissions at Kendriya Vidyalaya follow the official guidelines issued by the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS). Seats are allocated based on priority categories (Defense, Central Govt, State Govt employees, and General/RTE categories).

01

Online Registration

Apply online via the KVS admission portal during the spring registration window (usually March-April).

02

Draw of Lots & Lists

Review lists generated based on priority rules and RTE category seat reservations.

03

Document Verification

Bring birth certificate, service certificate (if applicable), and address proof to confirm admission.
